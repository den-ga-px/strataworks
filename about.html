About Us

The Team Behind
Every Fix.

Founded in 2012 and headquartered in Chicago, Strata Works has grown from a two-person handyman service into a nationwide maintenance company trusted by 1,200+ commercial clients and 15,000+ homeowners in all 50 states.

Our Story

Built on a
Simple Belief

Strata Works was founded in 2012 by David Chen, a licensed contractor who was frustrated with how fragmented and unreliable the property maintenance industry was. Property managers were juggling a dozen vendors. Homeowners were getting quotes that changed the moment a technician arrived. Nobody was taking accountability.

David started small — just a handyman service operating out of Chicago's North Side — with a simple promise: show up on time, charge the price you quoted, and stand behind your work. Word spread fast. Within three years, Strata Works had expanded to HVAC, electrical, and plumbing, and signed its first commercial maintenance contract with a 12-unit apartment complex.

Today, Strata Works operates nationwide with regional dispatch hubs across the country, a team of 400+ licensed technicians, and a technology platform that tracks every work order, every visit, and every guarantee in real time. Our mission hasn't changed: make property maintenance reliable, transparent, and completely stress-free for every client we serve.
