FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can you respond to emergencies?

Our average on-site emergency response time is 2 hours — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Commercial clients on SLA contracts have contractually guaranteed response windows. For residential customers, emergency calls are prioritized in our dispatch queue above scheduled work.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Yes — every Strata Works technician holds the appropriate state trade license for their specialty (plumbing, electrical, HVAC, etc.) and undergoes a full criminal background check and drug screening before their first assignment. Strata Works carries $5 million in general liability insurance, plus workers' compensation on all employees.

Do you offer maintenance contracts?

Absolutely. We offer residential home care plans starting at $49/month and fully customized commercial preventive maintenance contracts for businesses of any size. Commercial contracts include multi-trade coverage, a dedicated account manager, SLA-backed response times, and monthly performance reports.

What areas do you serve?

Strata Works operates in all 50 states. Our headquarters is in Chicago, IL with regional dispatch hubs across the country. Whether you have a single property in rural Montana or 50 locations across multiple states, we have coverage. Contact our team for availability in your specific market.

What's your service guarantee?

All labor performed by Strata Works technicians carries a 90-day guarantee. If any work we complete has a defect or failure within 90 days of the service date, we return and fix it at no charge — no questions asked. This applies to residential and commercial jobs. Parts warranties vary by manufacturer and are in addition to our labor guarantee.

Can I manage multiple properties through one account?

Yes. Our commercial client portal is built for multi-site management. You can view service history, open work orders, upcoming schedules, and invoices across all your locations in one dashboard. We also offer multi-site volume discounts — ask your account manager for details on portfolio pricing.
