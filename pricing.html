Pricing FAQ

Billing Questions

Are there any hidden fees?

Never. Strata Works quotes you a flat price before any work begins. You approve it, and that's exactly what you pay. Parts and materials are billed at cost with receipts — no markups. The only exception is a $49 after-hours emergency call-out fee for non-members, which is always disclosed upfront.

Can I cancel my residential plan?

Monthly residential plans (Home Basic and Home Plus) can be cancelled at any time with 30 days' notice — no cancellation penalties. Annual plans are non-refundable after the first 30 days but may be paused for up to 3 months at no charge. Contact your member support line to make changes.

How is billing handled for commercial clients?

Commercial clients receive a single consolidated monthly invoice covering all services, all locations, and all trades. Net-30 payment terms are standard. ACH, check, and major credit cards are accepted. Clients managing multiple properties receive one itemized invoice with line items per location.

Do member discounts apply to parts as well?

Home Basic and Home Plus members receive 10% off labor. Annual Plan members receive 15% off parts in addition to labor discounts. Commercial clients on Professional and Enterprise plans negotiate parts pricing as part of their contract scope. Parts are always billed at documented cost on all plans.

Is there a service call fee on top of the quoted price?

No. For all scheduled residential appointments, there is no separate service call fee — the price quoted includes the trip, labor, and any standard consumables. After-hours emergency calls carry a $49 call-out fee for non-members; this fee is waived for all Home Plus and Annual Plan members.

Can I get a quote without committing to a plan?

Absolutely. Residential and commercial quotes are completely free and carry no obligation. For homeowners, submit your request online and receive a fixed quote within hours. For commercial properties, our account team prepares a detailed proposal within 24 hours — no pressure, no commitment required.
